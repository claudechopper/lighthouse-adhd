## Step 4 — Pick one combination

Don't pick the most exciting. Pick the one with the **shortest path to a publishable artifact this week**.

**My pick:** _(e.g. ADHD research + micro-SaaS building)_

**First publishable artifact:** _(e.g. a single thread about an ADHD research finding I disagree with, posted Friday)_

---

## When to re-run

- Once per quarter, at the convergence-bet review point.
- Or whenever the current bet feels dead and you don't know why.
- NOT every week. Audit-shopping is procrastination.
